CAGAYAN STATE UNIVERSITY

Aparri Campus

Mission

Cagayan State University shall produce globally competent graduates through excellent instruction, innovative, and creative research, responsive public service and productive industry and community engagement.

Vission

CSU is University with global stature in the arts, culture, agriculture, and fisheries, the sciences as well as technological and professional fields.

College Goals

The College of Fisheries and Marine Sciences having been Center Of Excellence and Development in Fisheries and Marine Sciences.respectively shall continue to provide quality education and trainings to producethe needed manpower of the region as well as the country in developing the fisheries and marine sciences as well as the industry.

PROGRAM OBJECTIVES

English

1.Globally competitive graduates in the area of sustainable aquaculture, capture fisheries, post-harvest, and aquatic resources and ecology.
2.Fisheries professionals imbued with high level of integrity, nationalism and ethical standards; Leaders in their profession and respective communities;and
3.Proffesionals with knowledge, skills and positive attitudes in fisheries research, resource managemnt, instruction, extension, producton, and marketing.

Tagalog

1.Nakakasabay ang mga mag-aaral sa pandaigdigang larang ng aquaculture, capture fisheries, post-harvest, at aquatic resourses at ekolohiya.
2.Nagtataglay ng mataas na integridad, nasyonalismo at etika ang bawat propesyunal sa larang ng pangingisda.
3.May kakayahang sa sariling propesyon, at sariling komunidad;
4.Nag-aangkin and bawat propesyunal ng kaalaman, kasanayan at positibong pag-uugali tungo sa pananliksik sa ppangisdaan, pangangasiwa sa kayamanan, instruksyon, ekstensyon, producsyon at marketing.

SPECIALIZATION

Aquaculture and Aquatic Resources

AARM is committed to improving regional institutional capacity in aquaculture and aquatic resources management and related fields through innovative approaches that integrate education, research and outreach.

Post-Harvest

activities which take place when fish and shellfish are removed from the pond, cage or other production unit, and before the products are marketed for human consumption as food.

Marine Fisheries and Sports

physical and biological entities that are found in seas and oceans that are beneficial to man.
